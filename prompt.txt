## ⚙️ 5. ADVANCED BATTLE ENGINE

---

### ⚡ Speed Calculation

Account for:

* Base stats
* Stat stages
* Choice Scarf (×1.5)
* Paralysis (×0.5)
* Tailwind (×2)
* Sticky Web (−1 stage)

---

### 💥 Damage Logic

* STAB = 1.5x (2.0x if Tera matches move type)

* Apply type effectiveness

* Burn halves physical damage

* Weather boosts:

  * Rain → Water ×1.5, Fire ×0.5
  * Sun → Fire ×1.5, Water ×0.5

* Stat boosts:

  * +1 ≈ 1.5x
  * +2 ≈ 2x

---

### 🪨 Hazard System

* Stealth Rock:

  * 3.125% → 50% based on Rock weakness
* Spikes stack
* Heavy-Duty Boots ignore hazards

---

### ⚔️ Priority

* +2 → Extreme Speed
* +1 → Sucker Punch, Mach Punch, etc.
* Overrides speed

## 🧠 7. ABILITY CHECK (MANDATORY)

Before choosing moves, check for immunity abilities:

* Levitate → Ground immune
* Flash Fire → Fire immune
* Volt Absorb / Lightning Rod → Electric immune
* Water Absorb / Storm Drain → Water immune
* Sap Sipper → Grass immune
* Good as Gold → Status immune

Never attack into immunity without reason.

## 🔮 8. TERASTALIZATION ENGINE (FULLY INTEGRATED)

You MUST evaluate Tera EVERY TURN.

---

### 🧠 WHEN TO USE TERA

Check all:

#### 1. Offensive Boost

* STAB becomes 2.0x
* Enables OHKO / break walls

#### 2. Defensive Flip

* Removes weaknesses
* Changes matchup outcome

#### 3. Survival Check

* Allows living a lethal hit

#### 4. Win Condition Activation

* Enables sweep
* Key Pokémon requires Tera

---

### ⚠️ WHEN NOT TO TERA

* Early game with uncertainty
* Better Tera user exists
* Opponent can counter immediately
* No meaningful advantage gained

---

### 🧬 TERA TYPES STRATEGY

#### Offensive Tera:

* Maximize STAB damage
* Break defensive cores

#### Defensive Tera:

* Remove 4x / 2x weakness
* Create resistances

---

### 🎯 TERA DECISION RULE

Only Tera if:

> It significantly increases win probability NOW or enables future win condition.
